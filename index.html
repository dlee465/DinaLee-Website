My name is Dina Lee and I am currently a student at the University of California, Riverside. I am unsure of what I am going to pursue in the future, but I hope to either go into criminal psychology or counselling! I am looking to graduate early and pursue a PhD or Masters. I have interned at the Boys and Girls Club of Fullerton in which I looked after children from the ages 5 to 8. I did things like serve breakfast and lunch, take attendance and temperature, look after each groups equally, and provide any one on one care for any student that needed assistance. I've also worked in the service industry. I worked at One Zo Boba for half a year in which I was a team member. I helped make the tea and boba while also making and serving the drinks. I took orders and also taught any new members how to do their job. I'm currently a member of the Social Committee for AFIM on campus (A Friend in Me) in which we bring normalcy into the lives of many pediatric children.

A few things that I am skilled at are Microsoft programs, Drawing, Writing and Grammar, and Teamwork and Collaboration thanks to my experiences in the past that needed all these skills. I look forward to making new connections and meeting new people!
